Addressing SME Financing and Cash Flow Constraints

Bridging the Financing Gap for Indian SMEs

The Challenge:

  • Indian SMEs have a viable debt demand of approximately ₹43,00,000 crore (or $530 billion), as per RBI estimates.
  • Formal sources of credit meet less than 15% of this demand, creating a massive funding gap.

Impact on SMEs:

  • Over-reliance on informal financing options with higher interest rates.
  • Stalled expansion plans and reduced competitiveness in domestic and global markets.

Our Solutions:

  • Fundraising: Assistance in accessing capital sources such as private equity and hybrid funding.
  • Capital Restructuring: Optimize cash flow through debt consolidation and financial reorganization.

Succession Planning – Building a Legacy

Securing the Future of Your Business

Why Succession Planning Matters for SMEs:

  • Many SMEs struggle to transition leadership effectively, risking operational continuity and long-term sustainability.
  • Younger family members are often not interested in continuing with traditional family businesses and are leaving the country for other opportunities.

Our Succession Planning Approach:

  • Assess and align leadership structure with business objectives.
  • Develop and implement actionable transition plans.
  • Ensure continuity while integrating new leadership or governance models.

Benefits of Succession Planning:

  • Maintain operational stability and long-term growth.
  • Preserve institutional knowledge and strengthen stakeholder confidence.
  • Safeguard the SME’s legacy while ensuring alignment with modern business practices.
